Mental Health Day 2025

Today’s not about pretending everything’s fine. It’s about honoring the quiet battles, the invisible weight, and the courage it takes to keep going. To paraphrase a line from Tyler Joseph; sometimes to stay alive you have to kill your mind. It’s true.
If you’re struggling, you’re not alone. If you’re healing, I see you. If you’re holding on by a thread, that thread matters.
Let’s keep showing up—for ourselves, for each other, for the hope that tomorrow might feel lighter.
“My grace is sufficient for you, for My power is made perfect in weakness.” — 2 Corinthians 12:9
